## Interpretation

The forced smoke answers the control question: the declared
`algorithm_blueprint` surface is now exercised end to end through Scion's
proposal, Contract, Verification, canary, and screening path on formal CVRP.

The run does not answer the solver-quality question. Every screened candidate
failed `SCREENING_FAIL_WIN_RATE`, the champion stayed at version 1, promotions
remained zero, frozen budget was unused, and formal readiness stayed false.

Recommended next steps:

- Fix APS compactness/recovery first so completed and partial sessions stay
  within the observation budget after compact reads.
- Refine algorithm-blueprint audit/contract reporting so screening summaries
  include the full bounded `algorithm_*` audit fields.
- Clarify or repair `construction_keep_top_k` semantics before judging declared
  construction ensembles.
- Defer longer runs and deeper CVRP algorithm development until those
  observability and compactness issues are closed.
