## Interpretation

The primary blocker is no longer force-surface control, empty feedback
retrieval, Contract, Verification, canary, or selected-surface runtime audit.
Those parts behaved correctly and fail-closed where required.

The current blocker has two parts:

- Feedback attribution fidelity: APS feedback is now non-empty, but compact
  runtime highlights overemphasize representative/modal values. The agent can
  miss non-zero accepted-move distributions and cannot clearly see whether
  accepted moves translate into phase-level delta or final case wins.
- CVRP surface efficacy: `route_pair_swap` can accept moves, but accepted moves
  do not become stable improvement-loop phase benefit or case-level wins.
  `bounded_destroy_repair` still mostly fails to accept moves under formal
  screening, with `repair_budget_exhausted` remaining a major skip reason.
