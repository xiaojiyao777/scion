## Interpretation

repair survived a real forced CVRP campaign. The prior

generation. The system has a fail-closed guard, but the prompt still lets the
model choose from all surfaces after a forced surface is blacklisted. That turns
a diagnostic coverage run into repeated proposal failures and trips the circuit
breaker.
