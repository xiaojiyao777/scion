## Causal Diagnosis

The previous APS feedback/fallback budget problem is fixed enough for the
diagnostic path:

- compact feedback reads fit under the 48000-character budget;
- duplicate selected-surface reads are skipped or converted to missing-only
  fallback;
- forced `main_search_strategy` control remains stable;
- feedback and memory tools are still used.

The new blocker is C10 novelty persistence for singleton semantic surfaces.
Round 1's accepted hypothesis passed without a structured `novelty_signature`
and was later recorded as rejected after screening failure. Rounds 2-5 carried
materially different structured strategies, but C10 compared them against the
existing rejected empty-signature record, could not compute a semantic
identity, and fell back to strict target-file identity. That blocked all later
patch generation.

This means the run cannot judge the CVRP strategy surface beyond round 1. It
does not support long validation.
